Rossini

Fernando da Silva, Manager/Chef

From Peniche, a seaside municipality and a City, located in the Oeste region of Portugal. With over 20 years experience with cooking, from Cruise lines to Italian restaurants.

Big fan of fresh ingredients and cooking from scratch where flavour is a must, homemade dough and base sauces with the belief that dining out is not just about food, but a welcoming invite to come dine with me, where you will relax in a welcoming, friendly atmosphere with fantastic tasty food and delicious specials.
